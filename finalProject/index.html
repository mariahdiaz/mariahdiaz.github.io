Our Ever Growing World




By Mariah Diaz

Motivation

This project is intended to help one get a better understanding of the world around them and get perspective on how populated our Earth is. You will be able to see how population size varys around the world, see the change in population over the years and get a better understanding of how densely populated countries are in relation to their area.
